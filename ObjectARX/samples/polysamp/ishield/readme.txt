InstallShield for Polysamp/PolyCAD


This sample is to demonstrate how InstallShield can be used for installing 
ObjectARX apps for Demand Loading in multiple installed AutoCAD environment.

For modification of the program, it assumes familiarity and availability of 
InstallShield environment.

For more information, see comments at the beginning of setup.rul.

For demonstration, double click on setup.exe
