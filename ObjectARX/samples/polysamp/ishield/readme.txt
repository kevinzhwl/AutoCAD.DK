InstallShield for Polysamp/PolyCAD


This sample demonstrates how InstallShield can be used for installing 
ObjectARX apps for Demand Loading in multiple installed AutoCAD environment.

For modification of the program, this sample assumes familiarity with and
availability of the InstallShield environment.

For more information, see comments at the beginning of setup.rul.

For a demonstration, double-click setup.exe
